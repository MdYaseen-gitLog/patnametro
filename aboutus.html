PMet Route Map.

    Plan Your Journey

  • Select source station (Ex:Danapur Cantonment)
  • Select Destination Station (Ex: New ISBT)
  • Click on Swap Button to exchange selected source and destination station(if required).
  • Click on "Show Journey" button.
  • Showing all stations on map from "Source Station" to "Destination Station".
  • Click on Journey Info ▼ to explore your journey details.

    Enable Location(For finding nearest metro station)

  • Checkout the Enable location button in the Journey Info ▼ panel.
  • Nearest Metro Station highlighted on the map and showing in journey info panel, and automatically selected in source station.
  • Select only destination station if you want.
  • Click on "Show Journey" button.
  • Journey Info ▼ panel showing your journey details.

    Explore Map

  • Zoom in to map > click on (+) icon on map.
  • Zoom out from map > click on (-) icon on map.
  • Full Screen Map > click on (#) icon on map.
  • Change map theme > click on ($$$) icon on map and select desired map theme.(shown below)
    1. Street Map View: Google Map Street View.
    2. Satellite Plain: Google Satellite Map with no lable or information of landmarks.
    3. Satellite Info: Google Satellite Hybrid Map, satellite view with all information like google map.
    4. Light Gray: Light Gray map for light loading and data saver.
    5. DayLight Street: Daylight view of street map.
    6. Night Street View: Dark theme of street view map.

    Get Direction to the Nearest Metro Station

  • Select Route Map from menu.
  • Click on location icon in the map (*)
  • Showing your nearest metro station on map and direction to the station from your current location.
  • Click on map icon (*) to get Direction on google map.

    Measure Distance

  • To measure the distance on map click on (#) icon on map.
  • Click on Create a new measurement.
  • Click or touch on map for statring point of measurement and so on continuesly and finish measurement.
  • Distance showing on starting measurement on map.

    Downloading Route Map

  • To downloading route map. Go to Route Map or Station Details page from menu.
  • Click on link showing inside the map. Example:Metro Map 1
  • Map opens in a new window.
  • Tap on image and click on Download image.
  • Open your Gallery or File Manager to view downloaded map.
