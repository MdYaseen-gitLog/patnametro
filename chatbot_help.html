Chatbot User Guide

1. Introduction

This chatbot is designed to assist users by providing information, answering queries, and offering interactive features such as maps, schedules, and real-time updates.

2. Getting Started

  • Open the chatbot interface in your browser or app.
  • Type your question in the message box or use the microphone button for voice input.
  • Read the chatbot's response or follow the suggested actions.

3. Features

  • Information Lookup: Ask about specific topics, stations, or maps.
  • Journey Planning: Get directions, travel times, and fares between stations.
  • Live Updates: Receive real-time data for schedules, weather, or traffic.
  • Interactive Maps: View and explore maps for better understanding.
  • Quick Replies: Use suggested buttons for faster interactions.

4. Tips

  • Use short and clear queries for better results.
  • Allow location and microphone permissions for enhanced features.
  • Refer to example questions if unsure what to ask.

5. Limitations

  • Requires internet connection for most features.
  • Some services may be unavailable in certain regions.
  • Responses are based on available data and may not always be accurate.
